
# 📌 Project Overview

The Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project built to analyze sales performance, customer preferences, outlet efficiency, and product distribution across Blinkit's grocery network.

The dashboard transforms raw retail data into meaningful business insights using data modeling, DAX measures, and advanced visualizations. It enables decision-makers to track sales trends, compare outlet performance, and identify high-performing products and categories.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze overall sales and revenue performance.
- Compare outlet performance across different locations and sizes.
- Identify top-selling product categories.
- Understand customer ratings and product visibility.
- Evaluate the impact of fat content on sales.
- Monitor outlet establishment trends over time.
- Enable interactive filtering for deeper analysis.

# 📈 Dashboard Features

## 1️⃣ Executive Summary

Provides a high-level overview of business performance through KPI cards:

- Total Sales
- Average Sales
- Number of Items
- Average Rating

---

## 2️⃣ Sales by Fat Content

Analyzes customer purchasing patterns based on product fat content categories:

- Low Fat
- Regular

**Insight:** Regular-fat products contribute the highest share of total sales.

---

## 3️⃣ Item Type Analysis

Compares sales across product categories:

- Fruits & Vegetables
- Snack Foods
- Household
- Frozen Foods
- Dairy
- Canned Goods
- Health & Hygiene
- Meat
- Soft Drinks
- Bakery
- Seafood

**Top-performing categories:**

- Fruits & Vegetables
- Snack Foods
- Household

---

## 4️⃣ Outlet Establishment Trend

Tracks outlet growth and sales trends over time.

This visualization helps identify:

- Expansion periods
- Peak-performing years
- Business growth patterns

---

## 5️⃣ Outlet Size Analysis

Compares sales generated by:

- Small outlets
- Medium outlets
- High-size outlets

---

## 6️⃣ Outlet Location Analysis

Evaluates performance across different outlet tiers:

- Tier 1
- Tier 2
- Tier 3

**Observation:** Tier 3 outlets generate the highest sales.

---

## 7️⃣ Outlet Type Comparison
